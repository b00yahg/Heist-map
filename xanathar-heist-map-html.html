CENTRAL HUB
Multi-level with walkways

Central Hub

Multi-level circular hub with raised stone walkways over flowing water channels.

Contains pressure valve for water system that must be operated simultaneously with main controls.

Security: Regular guard patrols every 10 minutes.

Approach options: Move along walkways (visible), through water (concealed but poisonous), or from service tunnel.

WATCH POST A
Scrying Pool

Watch Post A

Contains a scrying pool that monitors the northern rooms (Intelligence Center, Storage Room, Sleeping Area).

Security: Staffed by goblin who sleeps 30 minutes, patrols 15 minutes.

Logbook contains guard schedules and security information.

WATCH POST B
Scrying Pool

Watch Post B

Contains a scrying pool that monitors the eastern rooms (Armory, Interrogation Chamber, Flooded Passage).

Security: Staffed by goblin who sleeps 30 minutes, patrols 15 minutes.

Logbook contains guard schedules and security information.

STORAGE ROOM
Uniforms & Replica Eye

Storage Room

Contains spare uniforms for Xanathar Guild members.

A replica of the stone eye needed for the Portal Room is hidden among the clutter.

Opportunity: Disguises available; replica eye works for one-time use.

INTELLIGENCE
CENTER
Records & Maps

Intelligence Center

Record room containing dossiers, patrol schedules, and surveillance maps.

Value: Information here provides advantage on stealth and perception checks throughout the hideout.

Security: Under surveillance from Watch Post A.

SLEEPING
AREA
Guard Zemk + Key

Sleeping Area

Contains sleeping quarters for guards, including a duergar named Zemk.

Intel: Zemk keeps patrol schedules and a key that bypasses traps in corridor to Interrogation Chamber.

Security: Under surveillance from Watch Post A.

WATER
REGULATION
Main Controls

Water Regulation Chamber

Key water controls:

• Red lever: Controls water level in Flooded Passage

• Blue valve: Controls water flow between Hub and Sleeping Area

• Yellow chain: Emergency flush creating diversion

Warning: Requires coordination with pressure valve in Central Hub.

Threat: Gray ooze inhabits cesspit below controls.

INTERROGATION
CHAMBER
Target Location!
Boss leaves prisoner
unattended briefly

Interrogation Chamber

Primary target location where Floon is being held captive.

Boss (Grum'shar) follows specific interrogation routine, leaving prisoner unattended for 10 minutes every hour.

Control panel on southern wall can reset surveillance system.

Security: Under surveillance from Watch Post B.

FLOODED
PASSAGE
Water Level Varies

Flooded Passage

Water level controlled by Red lever in Water Regulation Chamber:

• High: Hidden tunnel completely submerged (advantage on stealth)

• Medium: Tunnel partially visible

• Low: Tunnel easily visible

Contains a hidden tunnel to the Halfling Cellar.

Security: Magical alarm trigger needs to be disarmed.

HALFLING
CELLAR
Deliveries Dawn/Dusk

Halfling Cellar

Innocent halfling family's beer brewing cellar.

Regular deliveries occur at dawn and dusk, creating windows of opportunity with reduced security.

Connected to hideout by hidden tunnel from Flooded Passage.

ARMORY
Guard Uniforms

Armory

Contains weapon racks, complete guard uniforms, and shift rotation board.

Intel: Shift rotation board reveals guard change times (every 4 hours).

Security: Under surveillance from Watch Post B.

PORTAL
ROOM
Pressure Floor

Portal Room

Contains a portal activated by stone eye (carried by Nihiloor or replica from Storage Room).

Security: Pressure-sensitive hallway with pattern that changes daily.

Secondary control panel for surveillance system.

HOSTEL
CELLAR
Wererat Guard
Cargo System

Hostel Cellar

Base for Shard Shunners, a gang of halfling wererats.

Enhanced with cargo delivery system - crates moved three times daily.

Opportunity: Wererat guard (Roscoe) can be convinced to create a diversion.

Intel: Shard Shunners have rivalry with Xanathar Guild that can be exploited.
